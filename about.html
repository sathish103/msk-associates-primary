About BMSR & Co | Chartered Accountants

Delivering practical audit, compliance and tax solutions with friendly, partner-led service.

Who we are

We help companies, startups and professionals manage compliance and tax with confidence.

BMSR & Co is a firm of Chartered Accountants and finance professionals based in Bengaluru, providing comprehensive financial advisory, taxation, audit, valuations and compliance services to businesses across India.

With expertise spanning Direct Tax, Indirect Tax, Corporate Law, FEMA, RBI & RERA regulations and Audit & Assurance, we deliver end-to-end solutions tailored to the specific needs of each client. Our team brings together diverse qualifications and deep industry experience ensuring specialized, high-quality outcomes at every engagement.

We believe in a client-first approach: aligning our methodologies with each client's commercial objectives to provide practical, timely, and value-driven advisory that empowers businesses to grow with confidence and clarity.

Our Approach

  • Holistic Solutions - addressing business, commercial, tax and regulatory requirements together.
  • Commercial Efficiency without compromising Corporate Governance.
  • End-to-End Implementation including securing necessary regulatory approvals.

Vision

To be a trusted partner in business growth and financial excellence, delivering innovative, reliable and value-driven advisory solutions that empower clients to achieve sustainable success.

Mission

To provide exceptional financial guidance and advisory services that empower businesses to achieve sustainable growth and regulatory compliance with complete confidence and transparency.
