Managing Partner

CA Madhu Sudhan Reddy

FCA | CMA | B.Com

Madhu Sudhan Reddy is a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) and a Member of the Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICMAI). With over 6 years of post-qualification experience, he has built deep expertise across audit and assurance, direct and indirect taxation, and corporate financing.

He has served clients across diverse industries including Financial Services (Banks, NBFCs, Insurance, Private Equity), Real Estate, Manufacturing, and Food & Beverages. Passionate about creating simple, holistic solutions that address business, commercial, tax and regulatory requirements simultaneously, he is known for achieving commercial efficiency without compromising Corporate Governance.

He holds the 29th All India Rank in the CMA Final Examinations - a testament to his rigorous analytical foundation.
